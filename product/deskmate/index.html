Deskmate

AI-native app for finding places and people to co-work with — by vibes.

I initially built this product because I have a saved list of good places to cowork, but it's hard to search and share this kind of curation. Now with Deskmate, you can:

  • Search for a curated list of coworking spots with great vibes in cities like SF, NYC and Seattle
  • Share hidden gems or private spots for coworking
  • Get agentic recommendation based on your personal preferences
