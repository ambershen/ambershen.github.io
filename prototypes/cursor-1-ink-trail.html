Cursor Prototype 1 — Ink Trail

Move your cursor. It leaves a calligraphic ink trail that fades like wet ink drying on paper.

The cursor draws as you move

Inspired by East Asian brush calligraphy. The trail responds to speed — fast movements create thin, sweeping strokes; slow movements pool into thicker marks. The ink fades naturally, like breath on cold glass.

Scroll down and move around to see the full effect.

Speed-sensitive

Fast motion = thin trail. Slow motion = thick pooling. Like a real brush on rice paper.

Organic fade

Each stroke fades with its own timing, creating layered traces of where you've been.
