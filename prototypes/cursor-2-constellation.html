Cursor Prototype 2 — Magnetic Constellation

Floating particles are drawn toward your cursor, forming temporary constellations as you move.

Stars that follow you

Tiny dots drift lazily across the page. When your cursor comes near, they're pulled in — forming a web of connections like a star map. Move away and they drift back to their orbits. The page feels alive and responsive.

Magnetic pull

Particles within range accelerate toward the cursor, then settle into orbit — never fully touching.

Connection lines

Nearby particles draw thin lines between them, forming an ever-shifting web of relationships.
