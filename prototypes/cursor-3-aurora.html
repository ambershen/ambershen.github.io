Cursor Prototype 3 — Aurora Blob

A soft, morphing gradient blob follows your cursor with organic motion. Like carrying a piece of the northern lights.

Light that breathes

Multiple color blobs chase your cursor at different speeds, creating an ever-shifting aurora. The colors blend softly into the background — present but not aggressive. Speed affects intensity: move fast and it stretches and glows brighter.

Organic motion

Each blob follows at its own pace with noise-based wobble, so it never feels mechanical.

Velocity response

The aurora stretches and intensifies with fast movement, settles into a gentle glow when still.
