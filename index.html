Hi, I'm Amber. I'm building at the intersection of code, narrative, and aesthetics. I ship products from design-to-code, and bring products to the market from 0-1.

I'm currently doing technical product marketing at trae.ai, where we're democratizing software creation for everyone.

I grew up in China and spent my adult years between the US and France. Most of my energy goes into tech and art.

Some random stuffs about me:

  • I love making creative experiments with AI models, with techno music as the bgm
  • I consider myself as a modern renaissance person
  • I'm quite spiritual. I believe the frontier tech should be magnify human creativity and spirituality.
  • I take photos and paint, turning moments into memories.
  • I left my most reckless side on my snowboard.

Deskmate

I initially built this product because I have a saved list of good places to cowork, but it's hard to search and share this kind of curation. Now with Deskmate, you can:

  • Search for a curated list of coworking spots with great vibes in cities like SF, NYC and Seattle
  • Share hidden gems or private spots for coworking
  • Get agentic recommendation based on your personal preferences

Dirty Window

The most satisfying window you'll ever clean. Turns a mundane chore into interactive art. Wipe away grimes, experience realistic weather simulation, doodle messages on the fog, and capture it all in a built-in photobooth. Part game, part meditation.

The philosophy is to find meditative experiences from mundane life.
